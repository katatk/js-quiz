We Deliver Exceptional Service. The Result? Beautiful Hair.

Cuts.

Our master stylists can transform your hair from drab to fab. Whether you are after a complete style shake-up or just a trim and tidy. From lobs to bobs, long and flowy to edgy pixie-do's, our skilled team can sculpt your hair into a masterpiece.

Colour.

Colour is our passion. We can take you from icy platinum to deepest black and vice versa, not in one appointment of course! And any hue in between - Rich naturals to fun pastels, using the lastest techniques: balayage, baby lights, contouring, hand painting and ombre. If you can imagine it, we can create it.

Styling.

A great style can make your cut and colour really come alive and bring your look together. We are masters of the blowdry, and also style for formal events such as proms and weddings. We can make you feel polished and put together for any occasion.
