Upcoming Events

From the top of the North Island to the bottom of Steward Island there's always something happening - music, festivals, sports... you name it, New Zealand has it!

Wairarapa Ballon Festival

Solway Showground, Masterton, Wairarapa, Wellington

Friday 14th April

A magical evening for the whole family, the Trust House Night Glow is the Festival highlight. Tethered balloons light up the night sky in a dazzling display set to music.

Joseph and the Amazing Technicolour Dreamcoat

The Civic, Auckland CBD

Saturday 15th April - Sunday 30th April

A stellar international cast is today announced to perform one of the most enduring shows of all time, Joseph And The Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat when it tours New Zealand, starting in Auckland at Easter.

Lions Rugby Tour

Stadiums around the North and South Island

3rd June - 8th July

British and Irish Lions will 10 matches on their tour of New Zealand – including three Test matches against the All Blacks. It will be their first tour in New Zealand since 2005.

Dave Dobbyn Slice of Heaven Tour

ASB Waterfront Theatre, Auckland CBD

Thursday 13th April

An iconic kiwi musician, for four decades Dave Dobbyn’s music has become part of the nation’s soundtrack. He has won 29 Music Awards and three Silver Scrolls - more than any other Kiwi artist.

The Topp Twins Heading For The Hills

Matakana Country Park, Auckland

Saturday 22nd April - Sunday 23rd April

New Zealand’s well-loved national treasures are hitting the road on April’s Fools Day bringing their original style of merriment, mayhem, and mirth to the regions.
